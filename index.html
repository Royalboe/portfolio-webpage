About Me

Hi, welcome to my page, I am Ayomide and I am a physiotherapist, when I am not giving instructions to my patients, I am writing instructions for the computer to execute. I decided to make a career switch to software engineering because I find it so fasinating. I hope to make a very good career off it.

I am currently a student of AltSchool Africa and ALX. I am on the Cloud Engineering path. I chose cloud engineering path because cloud computing is an integral part of modern technology. Corporations as well as individuals are increasingly embracing 'Digital Transformation' to become more agile, competitive and productive. The cloud offers different benefits to different people or group of individuals. It lets businesses reduce cost of operation and innovate faster by allowing them access to secure, flexible computing and software from any location at any time at any scale they need.

My goal for the diploma program with AltSchool Africa is to learn about Cloud Engineering, get an internship to showcase what I have learnt and also secure a job
